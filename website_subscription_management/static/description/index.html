Now Host subscription-based services and products on your Odoo website with the Website subscription plugin.

The module facilitates the customers to purchase a subscription plan for products directly from the Odoo website.

The details of for a purchased subscription plan such as type of plan, due date, billing cycles, etc are visible in the customer's Odoo account.

It also allows the customers to View, Renew And Cancel a subscription Plans from their website account.

Basic Features
  • The module allows the Odoo admin to create and publish subscription-based services and products on the Odoo website.
  • The product page will display the subscription plans available and their individual descriptions to the visitors.
  • The customers can choose and buy the desired subscription plan available for any product(s) from the website.
  • The customer can view the details of all the purchased subscription plans in his /her Odoo website account.
  • A subscription service plan can be easily renewed and canceled by the customer from his account.
  • The customer needs to enter the reason and details while canceling a purchased subscription plan.
Subscription-based Product on Website

The customer needs to go to the product page.

Then. select the available and place the order to activate his/her subscription plan for the product.

The customers can manage their subscriptions

The customer can view his/her purchased subscription plans in the Odoo website account.

The current and previous plans are listed under the subscription tab.

List of Purchased Subscription Plans

The list shows the details of the purchased plans.

Details Such as type of plans, current status, date of purchase, etc are provided for a quick peek to the customer.

Detailed view of a Subscription Plan

Open any plan to view the details.

The status of the plans along with other details can be seen in the plan form.

Cancel your Subscription Plan

The customer can cancel a subscription plan anytime.

Open the plan and click on the cancel button. Thereafter, follow the steps to cancel the plan successfully.

Subscription Cancellation Form

The customer needs to fill the form to put in the request to cancel the plan.

The customer would enter the appropriate reason and any relevant comments and click submit.

Dependency

Dependency

Website Subscription Management Module is dependent on Subscription management. The latter needs to be installed first in order for Website Subscription Module to work properly.
